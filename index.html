Suryodaya English Secondary School is an English Medium, Co-educational instution established in 1986 A.D in Damak, Jhapa, East Nepal By the Nepal Jesuit Society. Suryodaya takes a keen interest on the formation of character building. Thus students are genuinely expected to attain a harmonious development which will make them worthy citizen of a country Nepal.
